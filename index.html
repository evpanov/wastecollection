Our Mission

Our main idea is to help our island in 2 ways at a time:

*

Make Cyprus clean from all the waste.

*

Give jobless people an option to earn money

The idea is elementary: we will pay any honest man 2 Euro for one big bag of collected trash. We are already doing this in Limassol in Pafos, but maybe, with your help, we will expand to the whole island!

This initiative is 100% crowdfunding.

We accept donations from everyone who can’t see how Cyprus looks right now.
